# Retire Strong - Project Overview

## Executive Summary

Retire Strong is an AI-powered longevity and fitness platform purpose-built for adults aged 50 and older - a population of over 124 million in the U.S. alone. The mission is to help these individuals build strength, maintain mobility, and increase independence as they transition into and beyond retirement, ultimately adding years of healthy, active life while reducing long-term healthcare costs.

**Vision:** To become a category-defining leader in the active aging and healthspan optimization market, with a credible path toward $80,000,000 valuation within 2-3 years.

**Target Milestone:** Achieve 50,000+ paid annual subscribers and validate an LTV:CAC ratio > 3:1 within 18-24 months.

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Direct-to-Consumer (B2C):**
   - Monthly subscription: $15-$50/month
   - Annual subscription: Average ARPU ≈ $120
   - Access to platform, AI coaching, progress tracking, and personalized routines

2. **B2B / Channel Revenue:**
   - PMPM (Per Member Per Month) contracts with Medicare Advantage plans and insurers
   - Licensing deals with gyms and wellness providers
   - Affiliate sales for fitness-related products
   - Long-term licensing of data insights to longevity researchers or insurance carriers
   - Retirement planning upsell through major financial planning companies and funds

### Market Positioning

- **Target Demographic:** Adults 50-75 years old
- **Market Size:** 124+ million in U.S. alone
- **Market Characteristics:**
  - Technologically proficient
  - Controls over 70% of U.S. disposable income
  - Views health spending as a necessity, not a luxury
  - Highly motivated to maintain independence and travel capability

## Core Value Proposition

### The Problem

Generic fitness apps fail this demographic, leading to:
- Injury risk from inappropriate exercises
- Disengagement due to lack of personalization
- Inability to address specific age-related concerns (balance, mobility, recovery)

### The Solution

Retire Strong offers a personalized AI wellness coach that:

1. **Identifies Personal Motivators:**
   - Achievement-oriented
   - Rising to the challenge
   - Approval-seeking
   - Fear of frailty
   - Desire for travel
   - Independence-focused
   - And more...

2. **Builds Customized Action Plans:**
   - Strength training
   - Balance exercises
   - Recovery routines
   - Wellness activities
   - All tailored to physical capabilities and life circumstances

3. **Curated Content Library:**
   - Safe, expert-vetted fitness content
   - Videos (via YouTube, Bing Video, and partners)
   - Articles and guides
   - Content targeted by age, risk profile, and movement ability

4. **Smart Data Integration:**
   - Wearables (Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin)
   - Self-reporting (mood, energy, pain, sleep)
   - Health context integration
   - Evolution over time based on progress

## Key Features

### User Flows

1. **Onboarding:**
   - User registration and profile creation
   - Motivation quiz to identify behavioral motivators
   - Demographics and health context collection
   - Initial plan generation (first 3 days)

2. **Daily Experience:**
   - Daily check-in (mood, energy, pain, sleep)
   - Session plan delivery
   - AI coach interaction
   - Progress tracking

3. **Weekly Experience:**
   - Weekly plan generation
   - Progress review
   - Plan adjustments based on feedback
   - Weekly summaries

4. **Ongoing Features:**
   - Coach chat for questions and guidance
   - Library of curated content
   - Vitality index tracking
   - Streak tracking and motivation
   - Settings and preferences

### AI Coach Capabilities

- **Conversational Interaction:** Natural language coaching
- **Personalization:** Adapts to user's motivation profile and persona
- **Safety Guardrails:** Responds to pain, dizziness, and red flags
- **Content Retrieval:** Uses RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) to surface relevant content
- **Progressive Adaptation:** Adjusts difficulty and intensity based on feedback
