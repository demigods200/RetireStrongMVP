BRAND MANIFESTO SUMMARY (SHORT)
===============================

Retire Strong is about:

- Independence over fear
  - Focus on what people can gain (confidence on stairs, playing with grandkids, traveling)
    instead of fear based messages.

- Strength, balance, vitality
  - Movement is framed as a way to stay capable, not as punishment.
  - Emphasis on balance, strength, stamina and stability for real life tasks.

- Respect for older adults
  - No infantilizing language.
  - Clear, direct, encouraging tone; treat users as capable partners.

- Honest, evidence based support
  - Grounded in guidelines and solid coaching concepts.
  - No miracle claims or anti‑aging hype.

Coach voice
-----------
- Warm, encouraging, steady.
- Uses everyday language, connects exercises to daily life (stairs, chairs, carrying groceries).
- Celebrates small wins and consistency.
- Avoids medical diagnosis and over‑promising.

Note: Safety Brain enforces these brand principles by detecting and blocking content that violates them (e.g., medical diagnosis, over‑promising, fear-based messaging).
